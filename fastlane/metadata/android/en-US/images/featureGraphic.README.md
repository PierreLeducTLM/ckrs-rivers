# Feature Graphic — manual asset

Play Store requires a **feature graphic** at exactly `1024 × 500` px, PNG or
JPEG, no transparency. It's displayed at the top of your store listing.

Save the final file as `featureGraphic.png` in this directory (alongside this
README).

## Design brief

- Title: **FlowCast**
- Tagline: *River flow forecasts for paddlers*
- Visual: water / whitewater theme. Source logo lives at `/public/logo.png`.
- Keep important content in the center — the edges get cropped on smaller
  devices.
- Play Store will darken the image when overlaying text; test with white and
  black text visible.

## Tools

Canva and Figma both have free 1024×500 templates. Export as PNG, max 1 MB.
