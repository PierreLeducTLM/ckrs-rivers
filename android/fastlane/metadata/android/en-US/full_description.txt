FlowCast brings real-time river flow data and forecasts to whitewater paddlers, kayakers, and canoeists.

FEATURES
• Live flow readings from public hydrometric stations (USGS, Environment Canada, and more)
• Forecasts that tell you when a river will hit paddlable levels — "should be good in N days"
• Favorite your go-to rivers for instant access
• Push notifications when a favorite river comes into range
• Detailed station view with flow history charts
• Clean, fast, mobile-first interface — built for checking on the drive to the put-in

Whether you're chasing a seasonal run, planning a weekend trip, or just watching the levels of your home river, FlowCast puts the data you need one tap away.

PRIVACY
We don't sell your data. Notifications are opt-in and tied only to a push token — no account required.

Built by paddlers, for paddlers.
